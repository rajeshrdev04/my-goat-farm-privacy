What Gets Deleted

When you delete your My Goat Farm account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (email, name, profile)
  • All goat profiles and records
  • Health records and vaccination schedules
  • Breeding and pregnancy records
  • Feed inventory data
  • Sales and expense records
  • Farm maintenance logs
  • All uploaded photos
  • All analytics and usage data

Data Retention Period

After you request account deletion:

  • Immediate: Your account is deactivated and you can no longer access the app
  • Within 30 days: All your data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Backup data: Retained for up to 30 days for recovery purposes, then permanently deleted

Delete Individual Data Without Deleting Account

If you want to delete specific data without deleting your entire account:

  • Delete individual goats: Swipe left on any goat in the goat list and tap "Delete"
  • Delete health records: Open the health record and tap the delete icon
  • Delete breeding records: Open the breeding record and tap the delete icon
  • Delete sales/expenses: Swipe left on the item and tap "Delete"
  • Delete photos: Deleted automatically when you delete the associated goat

What Happens to Your Google Account

Deleting your My Goat Farm account does NOT delete or affect your Google account in any way. We only delete data stored in My Goat Farm. Your Google account remains active and unaffected.

Can I Recover My Account After Deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted:

  • You cannot recover your account
  • You cannot recover any data
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use the app again

Alternative: Export Your Data Before Deletion

Before deleting your account, you may want to export your data:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Export Data"
  3. Choose export format (CSV, PDF, etc.)
  4. Save the exported file to your device

Note: Export feature availability may vary by app version.
